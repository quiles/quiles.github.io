Felipe V. Calderan

Ph.D Student

ICT/Unifesp

Felipe V. Calderan is a Brazilian Ph.D Student in Computer Science with CAPES scholarship, supervised by Prof. Marcos G. Quiles, Ph.D. He received his BS degree in Science and Technology, in 2021 from the Federal University of São Paulo (UNIFESP), Brazil, and is currently pursuing a Computer Science degree at the same institution. He’s a bronze medalist in the Brazilian Public School Mathematics Olympiad of 2012. His research interests include machine learning, clustering methods, neural networks and their applications in interdisciplinary problems. Felipe loves playing video games and playing the piano.
